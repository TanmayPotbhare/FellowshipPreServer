Fellowship Payment Information

Quarterly Overview: There will be 4 Quarters in a single year. Your fellowship will consist of a total of 20 Quarters over the span of 5 Years.

First Quarter: 1st April to 30th June

Pay Disbursement: 1st July to 7th July


Second Quarter: 1st July to 30th September

Pay Disbursement: 1st October to 7th October


Third Quarter: 1st October to 31st December

Pay Disbursement: 1st January to 7th January


Fourth Quarter: 1st January to 31st March

Pay Disbursement: 1st April to 7th April


Note: The Payment will happen in 7 Working days, as a result, the payment can go beyond 7 Days.

Please note that the payment is subject to the submission of required documents.
If the documents are not uploaded, the payment will not be recieved, you will have to visit TRTI office for further process.
