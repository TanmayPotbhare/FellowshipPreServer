IMPORTANT INFORMATION TO KNOW BEFORE FILLNG THE FORM

Basic Information
  1. After you press submit on Section 5, you will not be able to edit anything.
  2. Each section has three buttons - Previous, Save & Next, Next.
  3. Please select Save & Next to save and go to the next section.
  4. Please select Previous to go to the previous section without saving the details.
  5. Please select Next to proceed without saving the details.
  6. Fields marked with provide instructions on how to fill the field. Ensure to read them before filling out dependent fields.
  7. If the form submission fails, you can request a reset via the provided email ID below.
Information about Application Form Sections
  1. The form is divided into 5 sections.
  2. Section 1: Personal Details.
  3. Section 2: Qualification Details.
  4. Section 3: Domicile, Caste, and Validity Details.
  5. Section 4: Parents & Bank Details.
  6. Section 5: Document Upload.
Documents to Upload

Please have these documents ready before starting:

  1. Aadhaar Card
  2. PAN Card
  3. Domicile Certificate
  4. Caste Certificate
  5. Caste Validity Certificate
  6. Income Certificate
  7. 10th Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  8. 12th Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  9. Diploma Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  10. Graduation Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  11. Post-Graduation Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  12. NET/SET/PET/M.Phil Marksheet & Passing Certificate
  13. Ph.D Admission Receipt
  14. Guide Allotment Letter
  15. Guide Acceptance Letter
  16. Letter of Acceptance from RAC/RRC
  17. Confirmation Letter
  18. Research Center Joining Report
  19. Kendra Pramukh - Sanyukta Pramanpatra
  20. If Disabled: Disability Certificate
  21. Name Change: Gazette
  22. Acknowledgment of not receiving scholarships from elsewhere
  23. Research Synopsis/Research Center Allotment Letter
After Filling the Form
  1. After filling the form, you can download the PDF format.
  2. You can view your submission status by logging in. There are three stages to fellowship approval.
  3. Notifications regarding selection (selected) or rejection (rejected) will be sent via email.
  4. If you face issues, contact it_helpdesk@icspune.com via email.
